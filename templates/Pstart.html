PS 378 Survey

My name is Ben Massey, and I'm a student at the University of Oregon taking Political Science 378: Games in Politics. As a part of my final I've made this website in order to gather data.

This will be a survey. There will be a series of questions. Each question will provide two options, and you must choose only one. Each option will give you certain chances of winning or losing "points".

Treat it like a would you rather. "Would you rather have a chance to win 500 points in some game or a 25% chance to win 1500 points?" would involve weighing the pros and cons of chance versus reward for each choice, and then choosing which you'd prefer.

Do your best to act in the interest of maximing your points, like a game.
