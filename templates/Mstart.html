PS 378 Survey

This will be a survey. There will be a series of questions. Each question will provide two options, and you must choose only one. Each option will give you certain chances of winning or losing money. You must weigh the pros and cons of each option, and then make a choice. Try and act like you are doing this for real: like the money is actually at stake.
