PS 378 Survey

My name is Ben Massey, and I'm a student at the University of Oregon taking Political Science 378: Games in Politics. As a part of my final I've made this website in order to gather data.

This will be a survey. There will be a series of questions. Each question will provide two options, and you must choose only one. Each option will give you certain chances of winning or losing money.

Treat it like a would you rather. "Would you rather have a 50% chance to win $500 or a 25% chance to win $1500?" would involve weighing the pros and cons of chance versus reward for each choice, and then choosing which you'd prefer.

Try and act like you are doing this for real: like the money is actually at stake.
